Privacy Policy

App: Maestro Mobile  |  Last updated: March 2026

1. What We Collect

Maestro Mobile connects to your UiPath organization using OAuth 2.0. The app accesses:

  • Your UiPath process and instance data (read-only)
  • Your UiPath organization and tenant information

No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted by this app beyond what is required to authenticate with UiPath.

2. Authentication

Authentication is handled entirely by UiPath's OAuth 2.0 service. We do not store your password. Access tokens are stored locally on your device in session storage and are cleared when you sign out.

3. Data Storage

All data displayed in the app is fetched live from UiPath APIs. No data is persisted on your device beyond the OAuth session token.

4. Third-Party Services

This app communicates exclusively with UiPath's API endpoints (*.api.uipath.com). No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

5. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact your UiPath administrator.
